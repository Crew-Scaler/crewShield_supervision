## Research Organization Principles

### Selection Criteria

All papers included in this collection meet the following criteria:
1. **Minimum Length**: 7+ pages (ensures substantive research)
2. **Peer Review**: Academic publications or rigorous whitepapers
3. **Technical Depth**: Advanced security/ML concepts
4. **Relevance**: Direct or indirect applicability to centralized logging
5. **Recency**: Preference for 2024-2025 publications (unless foundational)

### Topic Clustering Strategy

Topics organized by functional aspect of centralized logging:
- **Infrastructure** (01): Collection, aggregation, normalization
- **Analysis** (02, 05, 10): Detection, anomaly analysis, behavioral baselines
- **Protection** (03, 08): Integrity, multi-tenancy, isolation
- **Threats** (04, 06, 12): Injections, attacks, evasion
- **Operations** (09, 11): Streaming, compliance
- **Supply Chain** (07): Specialized domain with extensive research
