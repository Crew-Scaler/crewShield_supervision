### 2. Data Consistency Models (50 papers)

**Focus Areas:**
- Eventual consistency vs. strong consistency trade-offs
- Conflict-free Replicated Data Types (CRDTs)
- Distributed system synchronization
- Replication lag management

**Notable Papers from US Institutions:**
- **ReViSE: Towards Reason-Informed Video Editing** (ArXiv: 2512.09924v1)
  - Self-reflective learning for consistency maintenance

- **Quantum Error Correction via Purification** (ArXiv: 2512.09745v1)
  - Novel approaches to error correction in distributed systems

**Key Insights:**
- CRDTs are gaining traction for conflict-free multi-region synchronization
- Hybrid consistency models (combining strong and eventual) are emerging
- Machine learning approaches to predict and resolve consistency conflicts
- Importance of application-specific consistency requirements
