### 5. Intermittent File Encryption in Ransomware: Measurement, Modeling, and Detection
- **Authors**: Ynes Ineza, et al.
- **ArXiv ID**: 2510.15133v1
- **Publication Date**: October 16, 2025
- **Pages**: 7
- **Relevance Score**: 9/10
- **URL**: https://arxiv.org/abs/2510.15133v1
- **PDF**: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.15133v1.pdf

**Focus**: Partial/intermittent encryption techniques and detection strategies

**Key Findings**:
