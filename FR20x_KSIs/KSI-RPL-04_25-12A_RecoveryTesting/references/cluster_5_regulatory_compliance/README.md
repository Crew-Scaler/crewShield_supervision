## Key Regulatory Frameworks Covered

### 1. DORA (Digital Operational Resilience Act)
- **Scope**: EU financial institutions, banks, insurers, investment firms, ICT third-party service providers
- **Effective Date**: January 17, 2025
- **Key Requirements**:
  - ICT risk management systems
  - Resilience testing (operational and threat-led penetration testing)
  - Third-party risk management
  - Incident reporting and management
- **Documents**: #4, #7, #9, #10

### 2. NIS2 (Network and Information Systems Directive 2)
- **Scope**: 18+ critical sectors across EU (expanded from NIS1)
- **Effective Compliance Date**: June 1, 2025
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Enhanced cybersecurity risk management
  - Incident reporting
  - Supply chain security
  - Continuous monitoring
- **Documents**: #1, #10

### 3. NIST Cybersecurity Framework 2.0
- **Applicability**: Global, all organization types and sizes
- **Core Functions**: Govern, Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, Recover
- **Key Innovation**: Addition of "Govern" function for strategic alignment
- **Documents**: #2, #6, #12

### 4. SOC 2, ISO 27001, GDPR Compliance
- **Scope**: Data protection, information security, cloud services
- **Integration**: Mapping between frameworks for efficient compliance
- **Documents**: #13, #14

## Key Findings & Insights

### Regulatory Timeline
- **January 17, 2025**: DORA operational requirements take effect
- **June 1, 2025**: NIS2 security measures compliance deadline
- **July 2025**: ECB to designate Critical Third-Party Providers (CTPPs)
- **Ongoing 2025+**: Implementation of threat-led penetration testing (TLPT) for systemically important institutions

### Stress Testing Metrics (from ECB 2024 Test)
- **109 banks** directly supervised by ECB participated
- **28 banks** underwent actual IT recovery testing
- **All preventive measures** assumed to have failed (worst-case scenario)
- **Focus**: Recovery capability, not prevention
- **Key Metrics**: Time-to-detect (TTD), Time-to-recover (TTR), Recovery Time Objective (RTO)

### Industry Resilience Assessment (Accenture 2025)
- **Only 10%** of organizations globally ready for AI-augmented cyber threats
- **81%** of security leaders expect cyberattacks within 12 months (HCL study)
- **54%** identify AI-generated attacks as biggest risk
- **76%** face high to moderate challenges in business recovery post-attack
- **11% average** budget increase expected in 2025 for risk management

### DORA vs. Traditional Testing
- **Shift**: From prevention-focused (defensive) to resilience-focused (recovery)
- **Threat-Led Penetration Testing (TLPT)**: Required for largest institutions, performed every 3 years minimum
- **Third-Party Risk**: Expanded focus on ICT service provider dependencies and criticality assessment
