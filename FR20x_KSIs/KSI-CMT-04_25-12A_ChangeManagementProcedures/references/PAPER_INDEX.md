## Key Metrics and Findings

### Response Time SLAs
- **15 minutes:** Critical incident response (industry standard)
- **7 minutes:** Mean time to detect phishing (Splunk 2024)
- **15 minutes:** Jenkins CI/CD polling intervals (proven in research)
- **24 hours:** Post-implementation review requirement

### Detection/Patching Performance
- **98.9% improvement:** APPATCH F1 score vs. baselines
- **1.0 precision/recall:** LayerCAM-AE perfect detection
- **15% automated fixes:** Google Gemini sanitizer bugs
- **20% coverage improvement:** RL-based testing automation

### Emergency Change Framework
- **3-7 members:** Optimal eCAB size
- **4 stages:** Prepare, Monitor, Respond, Improve
- **10 categories:** Incident classification
- **4 severity levels:** Risk-based prioritization

### Threat Landscape
- **15 days:** Average time from disclosure to exploitation
- **0.1% poisoning:** Sufficient for LLM backdoors
- **Weekly patches:** 2024 standard (vs. monthly previously)
- **72 hours:** GDPR breach notification requirement
