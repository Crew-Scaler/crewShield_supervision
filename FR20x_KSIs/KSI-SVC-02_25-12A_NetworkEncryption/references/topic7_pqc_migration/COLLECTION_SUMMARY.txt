================================================================================
RESEARCH QUESTIONS ANSWERED
================================================================================

Q1: What is the handshake latency increase with PQC?
    A: Typical 10-15% increase, but varies by algorithm (ML-DSA signature is bottleneck)

Q2: How much larger are PQC certificates?
    A: 2-4x larger than classical ECC (ML-DSA ~2544 bytes signature)

Q3: Which PQC algorithms are most deployment-ready?
    A: ML-KEM (lattice-based), Falcon (hash-based), with good performance/security balance

Q4: What deployment challenges exist?
    A: DNS fragmentation, DoS amplification, downgrade attacks, interoperability

Q5: What are the attack vectors against PQC implementations?
    A: Fingerprinting, timing attacks, side-channels (mitigated by masking-friendly designs)
