## Major Research Themes

### 1. Service Mesh & mTLS Security
Research focuses on performance optimization and security enforcement in service mesh architectures using mutual TLS. Key findings include comparative analysis of different service mesh frameworks (Istio, Linkerd, Consul) and their mTLS implementation overhead.

### 2. Certificate Revocation & Status Management
Significant innovation in hybrid approaches combining OCSP and CRL for scalable certificate status checking. Decentralized and blockchain-based solutions emerging for distributed environments.

### 3. Post-Quantum Cryptography
Forward-looking research preparing PKI infrastructure for quantum computing threats. Emphasis on maintaining operational efficiency while transitioning to quantum-resistant algorithms.

### 4. Cloud-Native & Microservices Security
Policy-as-code approaches for automated security management in ephemeral, containerized workloads. Token and credential lifecycle automation in distributed systems.

### 5. Certificate Issuance & Trust
Hardware-based trust mechanisms and attestation protocols for securing certificate issuance processes. Transparent and verifiable certificate operations.
