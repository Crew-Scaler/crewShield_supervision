================================================================================
KEY RESEARCH FINDINGS
================================================================================

CRITICAL VULNERABILITIES:
- Over-privileged agent access in MCP and A2A protocols
- Insufficient token scoping and lifetime controls
- Lateral movement via cross-app/cross-system boundaries
- Supply-chain risks in distributed service credentials
- Gradual privilege escalation through repeated low-risk operations

RECOMMENDED MITIGATIONS:
- Per-user authentication with explicit scoped authorization
- Ephemeral tokens with minimal permission scopes
- Containerized sandboxing with I/O validation
- Inline policy enforcement with anomaly detection
- End-to-end audit logging and provenance tracking
- Regular privilege review and least-privilege enforcement
