## Executive Summary

This research compilation focuses on **observability and behavioral monitoring of agent configuration changes**, examining how multi-agent systems (MAS), autonomous agents, and distributed systems can be monitored, traced, and controlled through configuration management and behavioral analysis.

The search encompassed three primary research directions:
1. **Distributed Tracing & Accountability** - Tracking responsibility flows and emergent behaviors in networked multi-agent systems
2. **Behavioral Baseline & Monitoring** - Detecting deviations from expected agent behavior through anomaly detection
3. **Semantic Drift & Configuration Impact** - Understanding how configuration changes affect intended outcomes

- **Category**: cs.MA (Multi-Agent Systems)
- **Relevance Score**: 205.0 (HIGHEST)

**Abstract**: Large-scale networked multi-agent systems increasingly underpin critical infrastructure, yet their collective behavior can drift toward undesirable emergent norms that elude conventional governance mechanisms. This paper introduces an adaptive accountability framework that (i) continuously traces responsibility flows through a lifecycle-aware audit ledger, (ii) detects harmful emergent norms online via decentralized sequential hypothesis tests, and (iii) deploys local policy and reward-shaping interventions.

**Key Contributions**:

- Decentralized intervention mechanisms for norm mitigation

## CRITICAL GAPS IDENTIFIED

1. **Real-Time Observability at Scale**: Limited work on observing 1000+ agent systems with sub-second latency
2. **Configuration Rollback & Recovery**: Insufficient research on automatic detection and recovery from configuration drift
3. **Cross-Layer Behavioral Correlation**: Limited analysis of how configuration changes at one layer affect behavior at other layers
4. **Distributed Consensus on Deviation**: Challenge of establishing agreement on whether behavior is "wrong" without centralization
5. **Human-AI Explainability**: Gap between mechanistic interpretability and human-understandable explanations
