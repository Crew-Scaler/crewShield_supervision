   - Continuous monitoring with feedback loops
   - Agent-driven compliance verification
   - Sub-millisecond policy evaluation

3. MULTI-AGENT ORCHESTRATION
   - Master-worker and peer-to-peer architectures
   - Standardized communication protocols
   - Distributed consistency mechanisms

4. TRUST & SECURITY
   - Formal verification of policy adherence
   - Symbolic reasoning for safety bounds
   - Cryptographic proofs in agent decisions

   - Violation detection and remediation
   - Integration with FedRAMP, SOC 2, HIPAA
   - Automated evidence collection

6. INFRASTRUCTURE-AS-CODE EVOLUTION
   - From declarative to agentic orchestration

   - Compliance-guaranteed deployments

7. GOVERNANCE SERVICES
   - Centralized policy engines
   - Distributed enforcement
   - Single source of truth

8. SECURITY AT SCALE

   - Policy injection attack prevention
   - Audit trail non-repudiation
